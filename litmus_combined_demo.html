Digital Twins04 / 06
Digital Twins — Contextualize Your Data
Data collected from DeviceHub and Analytics is mapped to a structured schema and matched to real-world assets. Machine, Line, and Plant context transforms raw data into actionable digital twins.
What are Digital Twins?
Join data from multiple sources and add organizational context. Map each data point to a real-world Machine, Line, and Plant. Build any data model needed for targeted use cases.
